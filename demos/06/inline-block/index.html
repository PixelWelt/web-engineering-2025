Cheetahs are fast-running big cats found primarily in Africa and parts of Asia. Known for their incredible speed, they can reach up to 60 miles per hour in short bursts.

Giraffes are the tallest land mammals, native to Africa. With their long necks and legs, they can reach high tree branches to feed on leaves and fruits.

Monkeys are omnivores, feeding on fruits, insects, and small animals, and they often live in groups that offer protection and cooperation.
